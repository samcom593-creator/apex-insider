## The setup

I'm Sam James. I'm 20 years old. I run APEX Financial — a life-insurance agency that recruits and develops agents nationwide. As of writing, we've got 70+ contracted agents and we're hitting $16-17K/day in team production. Personally, I'm clearing $30-40K a month.

This wasn't an overnight thing. But it was *fast* — fast enough that people keep asking me how.

This page is the honest version.

## The starting line

I started APEX Nation as an Instagram bio that said four words:

> I Make Leaders. DM me "APEX".

That bio out-converts everything else I've tried. Because it says nothing about insurance, nothing about money, nothing about "join my team." It positions me as the *coach*, not the *recruiter*. People who message me are already half-sold.

## What actually moved the needle

**1. Picking the offer first, the audience second.**

Life insurance is a regulated, high-AOV product with recurring commissions for years. One licensed agent producing $5K/mo gives the agency $30K-50K in lifetime override. That math means I can spend real time (and a little money) on every recruit and still win.

**2. Treating the funnel like software, not like sales.**

Every step of the recruiting flow is a system, not a vibe:

- IG bio → DM → qualification call → application form → license help → contract → onboarding cron → first deal celebration.
- Every step has a measurable conversion rate.
- Every step has an automation behind it (we've shipped 163 Supabase edge functions to date).

If the funnel leaks, I don't push harder — I fix the leak.

**3. Being annoying about quality.**

I track every "fell off" policy in a `policy_quality_flags` table. Right now there are 172 of them. Each one is recoverable commission if my agents reroute the client to another carrier. We attack this list every morning.

**4. Building in public.**

I don't have a moat. I don't have proprietary lead sources. What I have is *Sam James, age 20, doing it on camera*. The content is the brand is the recruiting funnel.

## What I'd tell a 19-year-old founder today

- **Pick the high-AOV regulated industry.** SaaS is sexy but the math is rough at your scale. Insurance, mortgage, solar, real estate — products that pay $1K+ per close and recur.
- **Build the system before you build the team.** A team without infrastructure is a chat group with extra steps.
- **Faith over hustle-porn.** I'm Christian. I read the Bible before I read PnLs.
- **Find one founder who's 5 years ahead.** Not a guru. Not a course. A person who'll text you back when you have a hard day.
- **Stop waiting for permission.** You don't need permission. You need a checklist.

## What's next

APEX is building toward 1,000 agents and $1M+/mo personal. The playbook scales. The systems are documented. The team is forming.

If you want in — as an agent, a manager, or eventually as a partner — the door is open. **No fluff. No application fees. Just apply, talk, decide together.**
